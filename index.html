What is a Balanced Diet?

A balanced diet gives your body all the nutrients it needs to work properly. It includes the right amounts of carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals.

Carbohydrates

Main source of energy. Examples: rice, bread, potatoes, and maize.

Proteins

Help in growth and repair of the body. Examples: beans, meat, fish, eggs, and milk.

Fats

Provide energy and keep the body warm. Examples: oil, butter, nuts, and avocado.

Vitamins & Minerals

Protect the body against diseases. Examples: fruits, vegetables, milk, and fish.

Water

Essential for life. It helps in digestion, circulation, and keeping the body cool.
